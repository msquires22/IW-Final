What is College Athlete Connect?
College Athlete Connect is a platform that connects college athletes looking to earn extra income by coaching with high school athletes and their parents who are seeking personalized coaching. Our platform handles the marketing and provides an easy way for athletes to offer their coaching services, helping them find clients efficiently.
Is there a fee to use College Athlete Connect?
No! College Athlete Connect is currently free to use for both coaches and athletes. As we grow, we plan on adding a paywall, so take advantage and spread the word now!
How do I find the right coach for my needs?
You can browse coach profiles on our platform by sport, location, experience, and rate. Use our search tool to find coaches who match your specific requirements. Once you find a potential coach, you can view their full profile for more details and contact them directly to arrange a session.
How do I sign up as a coach?
Joining as a coach is simple! Just click the Profile tab, create your profile including details about your sports expertise, coaching experience, and the services you offer. Once submitted, it will be visible to potential clients who can then contact you by the phone number or email you (optionally) provide.
Do I need to sign up as a high school athlete or parent?
No! Our platform is in beta and currently free to use, so just click on the Find a Coach tab and start searching!
